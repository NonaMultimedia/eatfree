How EatFree Works

A simple three-step process connecting hearts, vendors, and hungry stomachs.

1. Donors Give

Community members and businesses donate funds to sponsor meals. Every donation is tracked transparently.

2. Vendors Serve

Local township vendors prepare fresh, nutritious meals using funds from the EatFree pool.

3. Families Eat

Children and families claim free meals at participating vendors—no stigma, just nourishment.
